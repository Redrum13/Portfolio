Cartographer's Notes
  • In the beginning the focus is on the capital city region and the network connectivity. This is why you can only see the label for cities and lines for road. The green areas and water is also shown to give a topological reference.
  • After zooming in the focus is on buildings and road patterns. The zooming for roads and labels is controlled so that it does not crowd the map. The colour for green areas and water is faded out so it does not overtake the buildings and markers showing interest points. The width of road increases as you zoom in to make sure they do not thin out with increase in zoom and stay consistent.
  • Filtering is applied to place labels, poi labels, admin boundaries and point symbols to only show relevant information.
