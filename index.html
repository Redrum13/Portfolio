About

A student who loves maps

Erasmus Mundus MSc Cartography

I'm a master's student in the Erasmus Mundus MSc Cartography programme — one of the few joint degree programmes in the world spanning four technical universities across Europe. The programme has taken me from Munich to Vienna, Dresden, and back to Munich for thesis, each semester building on a different pillar of cartographic practice.

My interests sit at the intersection of cartographic design and digital technology. I care deeply about how maps communicate — who they include, what they emphasise, and how they guide the reader. I enjoy working across both the analytical rigour of GIS and the visual craft of map design.

I'm currently finishing my master's thesis and looking for junior positions, graduate roles, or internships in cartography, geospatial design, or GIS development.
