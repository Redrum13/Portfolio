I'm a master's student in the Erasmus Mundus MSc Cartography programme — one of the few joint degree programmes in the world spanning four technical universities across Europe. Master's Thesis · 2026
Thesis

Cartographic Approach to Remotely sensed Star Dunes and Environment Dynamics

TU Munich 2026 Katherina Anders and Pimmbhattra Maydhisudhiwongs Geovisualisation

This thesis investigates how dune crestline migration and environmental dynamics can be effectively visualized using remote sensing data and cartographic techniques. Using multi-temporal Sentinel-2 imagery and wind observations from the Namib Sand Sea in Namibia, I develop a reproducible workflow to extract dune crestlines and represent their changes over time. The research evaluates different spatiotemporal visualization methods, including approaches for communicating uncertainty and data gaps. The goal is to contribute a cartographic framework that improves the interpretation of dynamic desert landscapes.

In progress to fulfill the requirements for the MSc Cartography, Erasmus Mundus Joint Degree
